FAQ​

1.What is "Werewolf"?

Werewolf is stored on IPFS based on a collection of 10,000 unique NFTs on #hsc. Every NFT holder can enter the blockchain game.

2.What blockchain is “Werewolf“ using?

Hoo Smart Chain

3.How many NFTS?

There are 10,000 in total.

4.What can “Werewolf“ do?

Each Werewolf is a charming avatar and a ticket to enter our blockchain game.

5.Will there be rewards for holding NFTs?

30% of the mint fee will be allocated to nft holders, the more you hold, the more reward.

6.What is fomo reward?

The fomo reward is set to motivate users to mint, and the last mint user in a period of time will receive 80% of the bonus.
